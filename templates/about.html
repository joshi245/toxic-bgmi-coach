About RoastMyID

RoastMyID.in is a fun, AI-powered tool built specifically for the BGMI and gaming community.

Our AI analyzes your gameplay screenshots and generates a "toxic" but funny roast based on your stats vs skins.

It's built by gamers, for gamers, so you can share a laugh with your squad after a bad match.
